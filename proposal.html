Project Proposal

My website is about travel reccomendations to Taipei. This includes restauraunt and food reccomendation and fun places to go.

The objective of the website is to inform people about travel destinations, similar to a blog.

My target audience is young professionals between the ages of 20-30 who are looking to find a new place to travel to.

For my website, I will have the following content: one page dedicated to food spot reccomendations, one page dedicated to nightlife, one page dedicated to nature spots to travel to, and one page dedicated to city spots to have fun in.
